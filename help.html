Embracing Neurodiversity - Where To Find Support

Neurodiversity refers to the natural variation in human cognition and neurodevelopment. Conditions like autism, dyslexia, and others are part of this diverse spectrum. Embracing neurodiversity means recognizing and valuing the unique strengths, perspectives, and contributions of neurodiverse individuals.

On this page, we provide information about resources, organizations, and services that support neurodiversity and empower individuals with neurodiverse conditions. Our goal is to help foster understanding, acceptance, and inclusion, creating a world where everyone can thrive and contribute.
